Carpool Chaos Kerfuffle

  • CONTROLS W and D to move left/right. Space to jump. Hold W or S when jumping to super jump.
  • High Concept: Race Control a car and speed through an obstacle course
  • Player Experience Goals: Competitive Players should be able to handle a fast paced game. It's not too difficult to finish, but a highscore system separates casuals from the hardcore.
  • Genre: Racing Navigate your way to the goal.
  • Game play: Speed through a bumpy obstacle course and avoid slow pathways
  • Prototype goal: Game feel, making a racer that's fun to play
  • Features: Acceleration properly applied to racing.
  • Setting/Premise: A car struggles to overcome the nightmarish chaos dimension on a quest to the finish line. If you're not first, you're last.
  • Story: A car struggles to overcome the nightmarish chaos dimension on a quest to the finish line. If you're not first, you're last.
  • Target Audience: People who like speeeeeeeeed
  • Play time: Short, if you can handle it.
  • Strategies: Refine your path and reflexes by retrying the game.
  • Prototype Assets: Phaser documents online. All visual assets and voices were drawn and voiced by me. Music is from bomberman and Super mario World
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: Carpool kerfuffle and crafty the mouse.
