Dialbolic Kill-phone Kerfuffle

  • CONTROLS Arrow keys move left and right. Press space to hover with a mild descent. Press the up arrow key while hovering to boost. Down arrow key to Super Boost.
  • PRESS S TO SHOOT LEFT and PRESS D TO SHOOT RIGHT
  • High Concept: Flight Control a mech and shoot lasers, using boost and super boost as alternative speeds with appropriate cooldown.
  • Player Experience Goals: Moderate There's no real way to lose, but it makes for fun target practice. Controls may be difficult for some
  • Genre: Mech Sim Simulate throttle control of a mech suit capable of hovering and boosting. Shoot lasers at an arc.
  • Game play: Simulate throttle control of a mech suit capable of hovering and boosting. Shoot lasers at an arc.
  • Prototype goal: Tilemaps, enemy collision, death, and respawning, projectile sprites affected by gravity.
  • Features: A large world for target practice.
  • Setting/Premise: The player controls a cat piloting a mech suit and finds himself and Robo McSuit trapped in the bleak future, where phones rule the world.
  • Story: Robo McSuit aids our kitty on his journey to return to the past and save the future.
  • Target Audience: People who appreciate mechs.
  • Play time: Long enough to get a good handle on the game, maybe a few minutes tops.
  • Strategies: Release hover to fall quickly, line your shots up by staying slightly above targets.
  • Prototype Assets: Phaser documents online. All visual assets and voices were drawn and voiced by me, the music is from Bomberman Hero.
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: Reluctant cat-pack kerfuffle
