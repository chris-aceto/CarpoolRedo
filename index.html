Medical Drill Mech Kerfuffle

  • CONTROLS WASD to move normally, IJKL to drill through terrain! Press space to hover with a mild descent. I and O are lasers, Shift is a forcefield.
  • High Concept: Terrain Navigation Control a mech and dig through terrain.
  • Player Experience Goals: Moderate There's no real way to lose, but it makes for fun target practice. Controls may be difficult for some.
  • Genre: Mech Sim Simulate throttle control of a mech suit capable of dashing quickly through terrain, hovering, and boosting. Shoot lasers at an arc.
  • Game play: Dig through terrain. Simulate throttle control of a mech suit capable of hovering and boosting. Shoot lasers at an arc.
  • Prototype goal: Complex yet comprehensive controls. Tilemaps, enemy collision, death, and respawning, projectile sprites affected by gravity.
  • Features: A large world for target practice.
  • Setting/Premise: Robo McSuit and Cat-pack companion have been shrunken down to perform a careful procedure on the host of a phone parasite.
  • Story: Robo McSuit aids our kitty on his journey to into the microscopic world to disconnect these phones. permanently.
  • Target Audience: People who appreciate mechs and drills.
  • Play time: Long enough to get a good handle on the game, maybe a few minutes tops.
  • Strategies: Drill targets on the opposite side of terrain, shoot them when out in the open.
  • Prototype Assets: Phaser documents online. All visual assets and voices were drawn and voiced by me, the music is from Bomberman Hero.
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: Dialbolic Kill phone Kerfuffle
